# QUICK START - Emaan Mart E-Commerce Website

## 🚀 Get Started in 2 Minutes!

### Step 1: Open the Website
Double-click `index.html` and open it in your web browser.

### Step 2: Browse Products
- Click "Shop" or "Shop Now" button
- View all 30+ products
- Filter by category or price
- Sort by rating or price

### Step 3: Add to Cart
- Click "Add to Cart" on any product
- See cart counter update
- Click cart icon to view cart

### Step 4: Checkout
1. Click shopping cart icon
2. Review your items
3. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
4. Fill in your information:
   - Full name, email, phone
   - Shipping address
   - Select payment method
5. Click "Place Order"

### Step 5: Try Other Features
- **Wishlist**: Click heart icon to save favorites
- **Categories**: Browse by product type
- **About Us**: Learn about Emaan Mart
- **Contact**: Get support information
- **FAQ**: Find answers to common questions
- **Order Tracking**: Track sample order (ID: EM1234567890)
